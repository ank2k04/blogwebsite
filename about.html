HEEELLOOOOOO.....

Hello, my name is Ankush,. I had so many things that I thought that I loved, but the truth was I couldn't even appreciate any lf it because there was too much.

Things were terrible. I had a collage that I hated, a home that I didn't want to live in, and I couldn't hold a relationship.

BTW.. I'm an anime enthusiast with a particular passion for romantic comedies. Although I'm just an average person, I have a deep love for travel and exploring new places, especially when I have the opportunity to share those experiences with others. As an introvert, I sometimes struggle to connect with new people, but I'm always up for an adventure if it means getting to know someone better.

There is one secret I'd like to share: I have feelings for my best friend. Despite the challenges that come with these feelings, I am committed to being honest with myself and those around me, and I'm looking forward to seeing where life takes me next. Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me.
